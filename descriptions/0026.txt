Description

A list of all City of Edmonton transportation maintenance areas and the date on which each is scheduled for residential snow clearing. Does NOT include arterials or major routes.

Please contact 311@edmonton.ca for further details and/or explanations.


Columns

Maintenance Area Number	
Unique identifier for a particular Maintenance Area
Number
	
Neighbourhood	
Name of Neighbourhood where snow removal activity is going to occur
Plain Text
	
Scheduled Date	
Date when snow removal activity is to occur in the Neighbourhood
Plain Text
	
Status	
Status for snow removal activity in Neighbourhood. Note: TBD means To Be Determined
Plain Text
	
Scheduled Day
Plain Text
	
Location	
Centroid of the Maintenance Area (concatenation of latitude and longitude)
Location
