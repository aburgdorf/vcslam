Description


Traffic Intersections in the City of Dallas which have a reported accident count of 50 or greater during the five year period 2015 through 2019.



Columns

STREET_NAME	
Street location of reported traffic accident
Plain Text
	
STREET_NAME_2	
Intersecting street location of reported traffic accident
Plain Text
	
ACCIDENT_CNT	
Total number of reported traffic accidents
Number
	
INJURY_CNT	
Total number of recorded injuries
Number
	
DEATH_CNT	
Total number of recorded deaths
Number
	
GeoReference	
Geographic location of street intersection
Point
